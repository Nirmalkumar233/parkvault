Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") is an electronic record formed under the Information Technology Act, 2000. By accessing the ParkVault Platform, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal data.

Data Collection
  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email, mobile number with OTP verification.
  • Geolocation Data: GPS location used for parking navigation.
  • Vehicle Metadata: Vehicle number and type for automated check-in.
Security

Sensitive data is encrypted using SHA-256 security standards and protected against unauthorized access.

Third-Party Disclosure

Data may be shared with secure payment gateways and authorized supervisors for booking verification.

Terms & Conditions

User Eligibility

Services are available only to individuals capable of forming legally binding contracts under the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

Booking Contract

A confirmed booking represents a time-bound license to utilize a parking slot for the date and time listed in the QR ticket.

Fees & Taxes
  • 15% non-refundable platform booking fee
  • 18% GST (9% CGST + 9% SGST)
Verification

Entry requires QR verification by a ParkVault supervisor.

Limitation of Liability

ParkVault is an aggregator and does not own parking premises. The company is not responsible for theft or damages within third-party parking locations.

Return / Refund Policy

Perishable Inventory

Parking slots are time-sensitive inventory removed from availability once booked.

User Cancellations
  • More than 2 hours before start → 75% refund of base price
  • Within 2 hours → No refund
Platform Cancellations

If ParkVault cancels due to operational issues, a 100% refund will be issued.

Refund Processing

Refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Instant On-Site Bookings

Bookings created by supervisors via the admin app are non-refundable after OTP validation.
